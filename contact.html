Frequently Asked Questions

How does the mortgage application process work?

Our process begins with an initial consultation to understand your needs, followed by pre-approval, property selection, formal approval, and settlement. We guide you through each step.

Are your services free?

Yes, our services are free to you as we're paid a commission by the lender when your loan settles. We'll always be transparent about any fees that may apply.

How long does it take to get loan approval?

Pre-approval typically takes 1-3 business days, while formal approval can take 5-10 business days depending on the lender and complexity of your application.

Can you help with bad credit history?

Yes, we work with specialist lenders who consider applications from borrowers with less-than-perfect credit histories. Contact us to discuss your specific situation.
