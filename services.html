Our Loan Services

We offer a comprehensive range of mortgage solutions tailored to your unique financial situation and goals.

Home Loans

Finding the right home loan can be overwhelming with so many options available. At VnB Loans, we simplify the process by comparing hundreds of loan products across our panel of over 40 lenders to find the perfect match for your needs.

Our Home Loan Options Include:

First Home Buyers

Specialized support for first-time buyers, including guidance on government grants and incentives.

Fixed Rate Loans

Lock in your interest rate for a set period, providing certainty for your budget planning.

Variable Rate Loans

Flexible loans that may offer lower rates and additional features like offset accounts.

Split Loans

Combine fixed and variable rates to get the best of both worlds and manage interest rate risk.

Refinancing

Refinancing your existing mortgage could help you save money, access equity, or consolidate debt. Our brokers will analyze your current loan and financial situation to determine if refinancing is right for you.

Benefits of Refinancing:

  • Lower Interest Rates: Potentially reduce your monthly repayments.
  • Access Home Equity: Tap into your home's value for renovations, investments, or other major expenses.
  • Debt Consolidation: Combine multiple debts into one manageable loan with a potentially lower interest rate.
  • Better Loan Features: Switch to a loan with features that better suit your current needs.

Investment Loans

Building a property portfolio requires strategic financing. Our investment loan specialists can help you structure your loans to maximize tax benefits and long-term returns.

Investment Loan Features:

Interest-Only Options

Minimize repayments during the initial loan period to improve cash flow.

Portfolio Structuring

Strategic advice on how to structure multiple investment loans for optimal tax efficiency.

Equity Release

Access equity from existing properties to fund further investments and grow your portfolio.

SMSF Lending

Specialized loans for purchasing investment properties through self-managed super funds.

Other Loan Types
